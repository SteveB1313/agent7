What is Onboarding?

Advanced Onboarding allows you to create "WorkFlows". With WorkFlows, you can create filters for automatically onboarding current and future accounts.

For example, if a new privileged account is added to the computer, ServerA, you would normally have to log into Agent7 and manually onboard the account. A WorkFlow will continuously run and if a account matches your rule, the account will be onboarded automatically.

The most common use case is you may want to tell Agent7 to automatically onboard accounts that are added to any computers Local Administrator group.
